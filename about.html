About VoteBlocks

Elections are an integral part of any democratic institution. Therefore a transparent, secure and easily accessible voting system will greatly help in strengthening its foundations.

VoteBlocks is one such system ensuring Vote Integrity, Ballot Privacy, and Anonymity. All while being Remotely Accesible and Cost Efficient.

Integreated with Metamask, a cryptocurrency wallet trusted by over 1 million users worldwide.
